About Our Team

At Marugame Udon Brooklyn, franchise owners Amanda Phan and Ethan Nguyen are passionate about bringing authentic, high-quality Japanese udon to the heart of Brooklyn. With a shared dedication to exceptional service, community engagement, and innovative dining experiences, they strive to make Marugame Udon a go-to spot for fresh, handcrafted noodles. Their goal is to create a welcoming space where fast, affordable, and delicious food meets sustainability and convenience.

Our Goals

At Brooklyn Marugame Udon, we believe great food should be both sustainable and accessible. Our commitment to these values shapes every aspect of our restaurant, from the way we source ingredients to how we serve our customers.

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact through a zero-waste model. This means using biodegradable packaging, implementing composting bins in our restaurant, and actively educating both staff and customers on responsible waste disposal. By continuously exploring sustainable practices, we aim to set a new standard for eco-conscious dining in Brooklyn.

We want everyone to enjoy the Marugame Udon experience, whether dining in or on the go. In our first year, we will partner with leading food delivery platforms to ensure our fresh, handcrafted udon is just a few taps away. Additionally, we will enhance the Marugame Udon mobile app by introducing in-app ordering, making it easier than ever for customers to customize their meals, earn rewards, and track their orders in real-time.

By prioritizing sustainability and accessibility, we are not only serving delicious, authentic Japanese udon but also creating a dining experience that is mindful, inclusive, and convenient for all.
